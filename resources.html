Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the Bugema University Alumni Network?
Click the "Join Alumni Network" button on the home page or click "Sign In / Register" in the top navigation bar. Fill out your details and select your role as Alumni.
How can I post a job or internship?
Navigate to the Opportunities page. Once you are signed in, you will see buttons to post a new Job or Internship. Your submission will immediately appear on the board for other alumni to see.
How do donations work?
We use Flutterwave to process secure payments via Mobile Money, Bank Transfer, or Credit/Debit Card. Navigate to the Activities page, fill out the fundraising form, and click Donate. A secure payment gateway will open.
How can I connect with other Alumni?
Use our real-time Chat feature! You can send messages to the global alumni and student community. We also host physical events which you can find on the Events/News page.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, update your profile, submit a form, or communicate with us. This includes your name, email address, graduation year, and any career or personal details you choose to share.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Alumni Portal.
  • Process donations and send related information, including confirmations and receipts.
  • Send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and administrative messages.
  • Communicate with you about upcoming events, news, and opportunities related to Bugema University.

3. Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to process payments (e.g., via Flutterwave) or when required by law. Job postings and chat messages you submit will be visible to other registered users of the portal.
