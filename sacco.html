📋 SACCO Membership Rules & Benefits

All registered alumni in good standing are eligible to participate in the BU Alumni SACCO.

🥉 Ordinary Members

Minimum registration fee: 50,000 UGX. Monthly contribution: 10,000 UGX. Eligible for small facility loans and alumni network benefits.

🥈 Silver Members

Registration fee: 200,000 UGX. Monthly contribution: 30,000 UGX. Priority access to scholarship nominations and enhanced loan limits.

🏆 Gold Members

Registration fee: 500,000 UGX. Monthly contribution: 50,000 UGX. Seat on the advisory council, investment dividends, and naming rights on university facilities.

🎓 How Funds Are Used

Contributions go toward student scholarships, campus infrastructure upgrades, faculty development, and community outreach programs.

📅 Contribution Cycles

Members contribute monthly or quarterly. All contributions are verified by the alumni admin team within 48 hours of submission.
